Worldwide Airbnb Trends Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Airbnb listings, market trends, pricing, ratings, seasonality, and host trust across major global cities.

This Power BI dashboard explores Airbnb performance across leading global cities by analyzing listings, pricing, reviews, ratings, accommodation capacity, and market trends. The goal is to uncover key factors influencing Airbnb growth, customer experience, and host performance to support data-driven decision-making.

Tech Stack
1) Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization
2) Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
3) DAX – Measures and calculated metrics
4) Microsoft Excel / CSV – Data storage and preprocessing
5) Data Visualization & Analytics – Business intelligence reporting


Data Source
Source: Maven Analytics Airbnb Dataset

The dataset contains Airbnb listing information from 10 major global cities, including:
Listing details
Host information
Property types
Pricing data
Reviews and ratings
Guest capacity
Trust & verification indicators

The data was cleaned, transformed  and modeled in Power BI to create interactive business insights.


Business Problem
Airbnb operates across multiple cities and property types, making it difficult to track market growth, pricing trends, customer satisfaction, and host trust from a single view.

Key Questions: Which cities lead the market? How have listings evolved over time? Which property types are most expensive? How do ratings and seasonal trends vary across cities?

Goal of the Dashboard
To analyze global Airbnb performance and uncover insights on listings, pricing, ratings, seasonality, and host trust for better business decisions.

Walkthrough of Key Visuals
KPI Cards – Overview of listings, hosts, cities, property types, and reviews.
Listings Trend – Growth of Airbnb listings over time.
Market Share by City – City-wise listing distribution and contribution.
Pricing Analysis – Average price comparison by property type.
Ratings Analysis – Customer satisfaction across key metrics.
Capacity & Seasonality – Guest capacity and monthly review trends.
Trust Analysis – Host verification and profile trust indicators.

Business Impact & Insights
Identifies leading markets, pricing patterns, customer preferences, seasonal demand, and host trust factors to support strategic growth and decision-making.
